Objective of the game

Welcome to a fast-paced collection of mini-games where your body is the controller.

Each game throws a new challenge at you: dodge, aim, react, move... all to earn as many points as possible.
Play a quick session or chain multiple mini-games in a row.

At the end, your total score appears... now it's time to beat other players and climb the leaderboard!

Move & React

Use your body as the controller! Wave your hands, move your head, dodge obstacles... Each mini-game uses different body tracking to challenge you in new ways.

Cowboy Duel

Face off against an AI opponent in a classic western showdown! Wait for the signal, then draw faster than your enemy. Quick reflexes and steady hands will make you the fastest gun in the west.

Score Points

Every action counts! The faster and more accurate you are, the higher your score. Complete challenges, beat the clock, and climb the leaderboard to become the ultimate champion.

V2 Proposal

In the next version, we plan to add even more exciting mini-games featuring advanced body tracking with pose detection. Players will be able to jump, squat, and use full-body movements to interact with the games.

We're also working on multiplayer modes where friends can compete in real-time, a tournament system with brackets, and customizable avatars that react to your movements.

The leaderboard will be enhanced with weekly challenges, achievement badges, and seasonal rankings. Stay tuned for these exciting updates coming in 2026!
