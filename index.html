Objective of the game

Welcome to a fast-paced collection of mini-games where your body is the controller.

Each game throws a new challenge at you: dodge, aim, react, move... all to earn as many points as possible.
Play a quick session or chain multiple mini-games in a row.

At the end, your total score appears... now it's time to beat other players and climb the leaderboard!

Move & React

Use your body as the controller! Wave your hands, move your head, dodge obstacles... Each mini-game uses different body tracking to challenge you in new ways.

Cowboy Duel

Face off against an AI opponent in a classic western showdown! Wait for the signal, then draw faster than your enemy. Quick reflexes and steady hands will make you the fastest gun in the west.

Plumber Puzzle

Connect the pipes before time runs out! Rotate and arrange pipe segments to create a continuous flow. Strategic thinking meets quick reflexes in this puzzle challenge.

Color Lines

Match the colored lines as they fall! Step on the right colors at the right time to keep the rhythm going. The ultimate test of timing and coordination.

V2 Proposal

Introducing the Floor Gamepad! In V2, we're taking Color Lines to the next level with a physical floor mat controller. Step on the right colored shapes with your feet to match the incoming lines – it's like Dance Dance Revolution meets rhythm gaming!

The floor gamepad features four colored zones matching the game's lanes. When a line approaches, quickly move your feet to the corresponding color and step on the shape to score points. The faster and more accurate you are, the higher your combo multiplier!

We're also working on multiplayer modes where friends can compete side by side on separate mats, a tournament system with brackets, and weekly challenges with exclusive rewards. Stay tuned for these exciting updates coming in 2026!
